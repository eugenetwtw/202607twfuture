About the author & book

Eugene Chang (張渝江) is a civil engineer and writer based in Taiwan, with degrees in civil engineering and law (NTU) and an MBA from the University of Illinois at Chicago. He has written on drones, blockchain, engineering, and public policy.

Taiwan's Great Future — Switzerland Today, Taiwan Tomorrow (2020) weaves Swiss comparisons, energy and housing, healthcare and technology, and a memoir of an eighty-day legislative campaign.

This site offers short, shareable issue essays—not a campaign property. The author is not seeking office.
